Our Programs for 2024-2025

HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

For the 2024-2025 academic year, our HUMSS program continues to prepare students for careers in the fields of liberal arts, communication, education, and the social sciences. With a curriculum that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and empathy, students explore subjects such as psychology, political science, sociology, and literature. HUMSS encourages students to engage with global and local issues, understanding society from diverse perspectives. Through project-based learning and interactive discussions, students will develop skills in research, analysis, and communication, equipping them to succeed in various professions or higher education in humanities-related fields.


ICT (Information and Communication Technology)

Covering topics such as programming, web development, multimedia, and networking, the ICT strand prepares students for careers in technology and innovation. Students will engage in hands-on activities, learning how to design websites, develop software, and manage computer systems. With a focus on real-world applications and industry-standard tools, the ICT strand ensures students are ready for the workforce or further studies in IT, computer science, or other tech-related fields.


Home Economics (HE)

Our Home Economics program for 2024-2025 focuses on equipping students with practical skills for both personal and professional success. Students will explore areas such as culinary arts, fashion design, caregiving, and household management. The program also offers opportunities for TESDA certifications, ensuring students are job-ready upon graduation. Through hands-on training and real-world applications, students learn the value of entrepreneurship and self-sufficiency, preparing them for careers in hospitality, tourism, and other service-related industries. The HE strand is ideal for those looking to develop skills that lead to immediate employment or starting their own business.


General Academic Strand (GAS)

The GAS program for 2024-2025 is tailored for students who seek flexibility and a broad foundation in various academic disciplines. It offers a diverse curriculum that includes subjects from all strands, such as humanities, sciences, business, and technology. GAS is perfect for students who are undecided on their career path or wish to explore multiple fields of interest. With a balanced approach to learning, the program helps students develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills, enabling them to pursue any college course or career that aligns with their passions and goals.
