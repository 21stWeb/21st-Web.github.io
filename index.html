Strands we offer

Explore Our Strands: Empowering Future Leaders

At SHS San Nicholas 3, City of Bacoor, Cavite, we offer specialized strands designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in their chosen fields. Our dynamic programs foster creativity, critical thinking, and practical expertise, ensuring that every student is well-prepared for success in both higher education and their future careers.

HUMSS

The HUMSS strand in senior high school prepares students for liberal education in college. It covers a wide range of subjects, offering diverse perspectives on the world and its people, with a focus on developing critical thinking skills. More info. Subject.

ICT

ICT is a Senior High School strand that prepares you for a career in Information and Communication Technology. It equips you with advanced skills in computer systems, programming, creating web pages, and basic animation.
More info. Subjects

H.E

The Home Economics (HE) strand equips students with practical skills for livelihood projects and employment. It also offers pathways to TESDA certifications, enhancing job readiness and opportunities in various industries. More info. Subjects

GAS

Designed to prepare students who are more inclined to general areas of study rather than specialized fields, GAS will present the option for SHS students to take their electives from specialized subjects of any other strands. More info. Subjects
